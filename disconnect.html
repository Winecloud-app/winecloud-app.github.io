Disconnecting / removing access

You can revoke Receipt Router’s access to a QuickBooks company at any time:

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon) → Apps, or open the Connected apps / My Apps area.
  3. Find Receipt Router and choose Disconnect.

Once disconnected, the app can no longer read that company’s data, and the access and refresh tokens stored on the operator’s computer are deleted.
