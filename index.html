Receipt Router

A private bookkeeping tool operated by Betten Wines.

Receipt Router is an internal tool used by Betten Wines to triage its own purchase receipts. It connects to Betten Wines’ own QuickBooks Online companies on a read-only basis to check whether a receipt has already been recorded, so the same receipt is not entered twice across the two businesses’ books.

It is not a public service and is not offered to or used by anyone outside Betten Wines.
