Privacy Policy

Receipt Router · Operated by Betton Wines · Last updated: 29 June 2026

Receipt Router (“the app”) is a private internal tool operated by Betton Wines (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what the app does with data. The app is used solely by Betton Wines to manage its own bookkeeping; it is not offered to the public and has no other users.

What the app accesses

With your authorisation, the app connects to your own QuickBooks Online company or companies through Intuit’s official API. It reads accounting transaction details — such as amount, date and supplier/vendor name — in order to check whether a given purchase receipt has already been recorded.

Read-only

The app only reads data from QuickBooks. It does not create, modify or delete any records in QuickBooks.

What it stores, and where

The app runs on the operator’s own computer. The only data it stores are the OAuth access and refresh tokens needed to maintain the QuickBooks connection, held in a permission-restricted file on that computer. Receipt images supplied by the operator are stored in a local folder on that same computer. No accounting data or receipt data is transmitted to any third party.

What it does not do

  • It does not sell, rent or share data with anyone.
  • It does not use data for advertising or profiling.
  • It does not collect data from any person other than the operator.

Removing access

Access can be revoked at any time from within QuickBooks Online (Apps → Connected apps) or by disconnecting the app. See Disconnecting / removing access. On disconnection the stored tokens are deleted.
